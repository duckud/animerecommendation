Assassination Classroom

This anime is about a class of students whose job is to kill their teacher, who is a really strong, octopus-looking creature. Even though their job is to kill the teacher, all of the students grow to love him as he is an amazing teacher and loves all of his students. It's a lighthearted and funny anime, that has 2 seasons and 1 movie recap. I like it because it was one of my first animes and the story is well written. It's really funny regardless of the bizarre plot and really easy to watch. Also I really like the relationship between the teacher and his students. (Cover picture source)

Attack on titan

In this story there are giant creatures known as Titans, that attack the villagers of a certain island and eat them. A young boy and his 2 best friends, who are really interested in the world outside of the walls that keep them save form the titans decide to join the military and fight against the walls, for both revenge and freedom. While fighting against the Titans, the military also tries to figure out what these creatures are thinking and where they're coming from. It sounds like a really weird anime, but it's extremely well-written. Not only does it have incredible plot twists, but also amazing characters and a good storyline. I must warn that it's quite graphic and sometimes even disturbing, but it's absolutely worth watching. (Cover picture source)

The case study of Vanitas

The vampires of this world are threatened, because some creature is stealing their real names, which is something that should never be revealed or they will be cursed. Noé, who is a young vampire whose close friend was a victim of these attacks, goes to Paris in hopes to find The Book Of Vanitas, which is a book that can lift the curse if used correctly. There he finds Vanitas, a human who claims to be a vampire doctor. Vanitas is the owner of this book and he is also trying to save the vampires from the curses. Noé and Vanitas start working together since their mutual goal is to save vampires. I really like the dynamic between Noé and Vanitas, they're the typical sun and moon combination. One of them is tall and dresses fully white and he's a really sweet and caring characters who believes in justice and wants everyone to be happy, Vanitas is short and dresses fully black and he's cold and uninterested and he doesn't care who gets hurt as long as he gets what he wants. You can probably imagine how Noé helps Vanitas become more open and Vanitas protects and has a soft spot for him. Not only that, but it's an anime with a lot of fights but it's also really funny and has romance aswell. (Cover picture source)
