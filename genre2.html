Anohana

After the death of Meiko Honma her friend group started drifting apart from the shock of her death. Ten years later, she suddenly reappeared as a ghost that only Jintan, the former leader of the group, can see. For her to be able to move on to the afterlife Jintan has to fulfill her last wish, the issue being that she forgot what her wish was. Therefore it's up to her former friends to figure out what her wish was and make it possible for her to rest in peace. It's a rather sad story, which I absolutely love in romance, but the story is also really cute and the friendship between them is also really sweet. (Cover picture source)

A Silent Voice

Content warning: suicide, bullying.
The popular Shouya was bullying a deaf girl who freshly transferred into his class. While Shouya continues bullying her, his classmate start turning their backs on him. When the girl transfers schools, he becomes a depressed and lonely outcast. When he finds the girl again after a few years, he tries to make the bullying up to her, as he regrets his actions. To me this movie shows how people can learn from their actions and grow as a person. I really like the characters and I also like that Shouya decided to make it up to her instead of trying to forget what happened. It's a really sad and beautiful story. It's also adapted from a real life experience, which just makes it more realistic and therefore beautiful in my opinion. (Cover picture source)

Blue Spring Ride

At the end of her first high school year, Futaba encounters her first love, Tanaka Kou, who had transferred schools 3 years ago, before she got to confess to him. After meeting him again, Futaba realizes that he has changed a lot. He got his last name changed and also acts differently. Gradually the two come closer again while figuring out what happened during the 3 years that they haven't seen eachother. I like how the male lead is like a completely different person which forces the female lead to get to know him again. But even though he changed so much he obviously still has a soft spot for her which is really cute to me. I like that she takes her time to understand him and to help him open up to her. (Cover picture source)

Fruits Basket

Content warning: child abuse.
After a family tragedy Tohru Honda was left living in a tent in a forest. The tent was soon found by the Sohma family members, which caused in her living together with Yuki, Kyo and Shigure Sohma. However, she quickly learns that the family has an unique secret that they've been hiding: they turn into an animal of the zodiac when they hug someone of the opposite sex. I considered putting this one in the psychological category, since the romance only really starts after a few seasons and the first few seasons are more concentrated on the trauma that each of the characters had. But since it is some sort of reverse harem, I decided to put it in the romance category. I like that each of the characters have their own issues and hardships. I also really like how the female lead tries her best to help all of them feel better about themselves. It's not solely concentrated on the romance which is something I really like in romance animes. (Cover picture source)

Given

Content warning: suicide.
The tired Uenoyoma wanted to take a nap at his favourite spot in school during break. But when he arrived there the spot was already occupied from the sad Mafuyu and his guitar with the broken strings. Instead of sleeping Uenoyoma fixes Mafuyus guitar. As soon as Mafuyu notices that Uenoyoma can play the guitar he begs him to teach him how to play it aswell. I really love all the characters in this anime. I like how Mafuyu has a complicated past and I like how Uenoyoma is the first one to fall in love and struggle with it. It's a really cute but also sad story. (Cover picture source)

Horimiya

Hori and Miyamura are both leading a double life: the popular Hori, who is kind of a mess and cares for her brother while her parents are at work and the quiet Miyamura who actually has a bunch of piercings and tattoos. They never really interacted in class until they accidentally discovered eachothers secret. They quickly became close friends and started spending a lot of time with eachother afterwards. This anime has a lot of couples an they all have their own unique relationship which makes it really interesting. It's a really lighthearted and funny anime to watch with really cute characters. It's also not concentarted on one couple alone which I like in romance anime. (Cover picture source)

To The Forest Of Firefly Lights

While visiting her uncle in summer a young girl gets lost in the woods alone. As she's trying to find her way back she's rescued by a gentle youkai named Gin. He's burdened with a curse that doesn't allow him to touch any human, since he would disappear if he did so. Even though they can't have physical contact they become really close and start meeting eachother every summer. I really like the idea of a romance where the main characters can't touch eachother even when they're right next to eachother. It's a really bittersweet anime but it's definetly worth a watch. (Cover picture source)

Howls Moving Castle

Sophie was living an ordinary life. After an encounter with Howl, who was rumoured to be an evil wizard, the poor girl found herself getting cursed by a witch. The curse made her look like an old woman, which resulted in her trying to find Howl to break the curse. Aside from being a studio ghilbi movie, I really like the characters and I like the idea of a curse. I also really like that Howl is rumoured to be really evil but in reality he's a really sweet person. (Cover picture source)

I Want To Eat Your Pancreas

An unnamed protagonist happens to find a diary that belongs to his classmate Sakura Yamauchi. She is revealed to be suffering from an illness in her pancres, which resulted in her only having a few more months to live. After the protagonist promises to keep this illness a secret they decide to spend her last few months together, despite of the fact that they were polar opposites. I really like the idea of a love story that is impossible from the start. It's a really bittersweet movie that is cute but really sad at the same time. (Cover picture source)

The Stranger By The Shore

Shun Hashimoto, an aspiring novelist who lives on a small island, sees Mio Chibana, a young and sad student, sitting by himself on a bench every night. Shun approaches him, as he is interested in Mio, and day by day they start growing closer. One day Mio decides to leave the island. 3 years later he returns though and confesses his love to Shun. However, when it actually comes to dating Mio, Shun gets scared. It's a really short story but it involves internalised homophobia, which is something that a lot of gay people struggle with, so I really like seeing a story where he can start accepting himself as he is. (Cover picture source)

Your Lie In April

Kousei Arima was a genuis pianist until his mother died, which took his ability to hear the piano. However, when he meets a violinist with the opposite playing style as him, he finds himself being ready to play the piano again. I absolutely love the idea of the "she saved me" trope. But aside from that, the story is really good and the characters are cute and I really like it. (Cover picture source)

Wotakoi: Love Is Hard For An Otaku

Narumi Momose struggles with relationships because every boyfriend she had dumped her for being an otaku. She was completely hopeless until her childhood best friend, who is also an otaku, asked her out and they started dating. I like that it's more of a mature relationship. It's about an office couple so they're all adults already. But I also like the fact that they can be true to themselves around eachother and I also really like that they're childhood bestfriends. (Cover picture source)

Your Name

Mitsuha and Taki are complete strangers who live really different lives in different places. When Mitsuha makes a wish to leave the countryside that she lives in and go to tokyo, they got connected. She dreams that she is Taki and Taki dreams that he is Mitsuha. They later find out that they're actually switching bodies. It's a really odd story that I've never heard of before so I really like. It's also a really famous movie and it's very well written. (Cover picture source)
