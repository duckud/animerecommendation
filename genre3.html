Banana Fish

Content warning: rape, prostitution, child pornography, child abuse.
Ash grew up in a really tough environment where he was repetitively raped and adopted as the heir of "Papa" Dino Golzine where he was forced into prostitution. When he turned 17 he forsake the place where he was living in. However, he was driven back to that place because the secret that drove Ash's brother mad during war linked back to Dino Golzine. Exactly around that time Eiji Okumura, a 19 year old guy that came from japan to accompany a journalist, became Ash's acquintance and got mixed up in that mess. This is definetly my favourite anime because it includes a lot of different things like rape, child pornography, trauma et cetera. Nevertheless, Ash was able to open up to Eiji and they got really close really fast which is really fun to watch. They're completely different people with different experiences and therefore it's interesting to see them together. The story is also really good and I also like the characters. Violet Evergarden

Violet Evergarden always had incredible combat powers, which was the reason she ended up being used as a mere weapon by the military. Her only friend and protector during this time was Major Gilbert, who took great care of her and taught her a lot of things. But during a war Gilbert died, only leaving her the words "I love you". Violet, who was totally clueless as to what that was supposed to mean, decided to become an auto memory doll and write letters for others after war was over. She thought this way she could learn more about others' emotions and would be able to understand what the Major meant when he said those words. This is a really bittersweet and cute story to watch. It's a rather short anime but I really liked watching her becoming a skilled auto memory doll and eventually understanding what the Major meant. (Cover picture source)
