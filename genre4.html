Angels Of Death

Content warning: murder, child abuse.
A young girl named Ray woke up in a strange building where she found herself getting chased by a serial killer right off the bat. After escaping from said serial killer, she was forced to face a truth she'd rather had forgotten which results in her begging the serial killer from before to kill her. They make a promise and agree that he will only kill her if she helps him get out of that building. With that, they both figth together to get out of the building filled with serial killers. I really like the friendship between Ray and the serial killer whose name is Zack. Even though they have a really weird promise, they really care about eachother which is nice to watch. Death Note

Light Yagami, who was an ordinary guy, found a weird notebook laying on the floor one day. The notebook claimed that if you write someones name in the book while thinking about that persons face, the person will die from a heart attack, if it is not specified how the person should die. With this book Light had the power to punish criminals. His goal was to make people stop comitting crimes by showing them that every criminal gets killed and therefore making the possible criminals fear it. However, a really successfull detective takes it upon him to find out the identity of the person behind the notebook. This anime has a lot of tension because both the detective "L" and Light, who's also called "Kira" by the public are really smart. While L is trying to prove that Light is Kira, Light has to look innocent and punish the criminals at the same time. It's really intersting and the characters are really intersting aswell. (Cover picture source)

Devilman: Crybaby

Content warning: explicit sex, drug use, sexual abuse.
Akira Fudo is told from his best friend that an ancient race of demons has returned on earth to take back the world from humans and the only way to defeat those demons would be to unite himself with a demon and fight against them. Akira succeeds in uniting himself with a demon, which gives him the abilities of a demon and the soul of a human. I really like Akira and Ryo's friendship and the plot is really intersting. I feel like many people don't understand that the story repeats itself over and over because Ryo, the best friend, keeps making the same mistake. It has some weird scenes especially in the beginning but the story overall is really good and worth a watch. ( Cover picture source )
